## Setup your account

Get started by creating an account on Snip.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="user" title="Create an account">
    Go to the [signup page](https://snip.com/login) and sign in with your Google account.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="credit-card" title="Choose a plan">
    Select a plan that fits your needs. You can start with the **Free** plan and upgrade later.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Create your first link

Once you're logged in, you can create your first short link.

1. Click on the **New Link** button in the dashboard.
2. Enter the long URL you want to shorten.
3. (Optional) Enter a custom alias (e.g. `snip.com/my-link`).
4. Click **Create Link**.

<Note>
  Custom aliases are available on the **Pro** and **Business** plans.
</Note>

## Track performance

Go to the **Links** page to see all your links. Click on a link to view detailed analytics, including:

- Total clicks
- Unique visitors
- Top locations
- Device breakdown
